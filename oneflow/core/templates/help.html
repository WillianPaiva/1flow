{% filter markdown %}{% blocktrans %} ## Google Reader import The Google Reader import takes places in the background. You can safely logout of **1flow** or close your browser window without disrupting it. Importing data will take longer if many users are importing at once as we have finite computing resources. Your import status is updated every 5 seconds. Upon completion, the number of imported articles may be less than the number of processed articles due to duplicate content in Google data. About the approximate ETA… Well… Given the nature of the data we fetch from Google, it's **very** approximate. Don't rely on it; we don't do. The import will probably end before the ETA. It pleased our developer to display it. {% endblocktrans %}{% endfilter %}
